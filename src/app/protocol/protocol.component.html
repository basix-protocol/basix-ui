$BASX is the token of the protocol. Every 24 hours, the Basix Protocol adjusts the total amount of $BASX tokens to target the value of 1 $BASX to 1 $sUSD.

These balance changes apply to all $BASX holders, but if you own 5% of the supply, you will never own less than the 5% of the supply, regardless of the amount of tokens you hold.

In each transaction (trade or transfer) of $BASX 1% of the transaction value is burned. This results in each holder seeing an increase in the % of supply that they control just by holding. Frictionless passive income to all holders!

The rebase process in depth

Anyone can trigger a rebase event using our DAPP. To trigger it the smart contracts needs to meet certain conditions:

- Current time must be between 9 AM and 10 AM UTC

- 24 hours must have passed since the last rebase

- Price difference between $BASX and sUSD must be more than 5%

On every rebase the smart contract does the following operation: totalSupply * (rate - targetRate) / targetRate.

Rate is the price at uniswap and targetRate is 10^18.

After that operation the protocol apply a 10% rebase lag, and thats the token balance that will change for everyone.

If you have 500 $BASX tokens worth 400 sUSD, your holding value will still be 400 sUSD after the rebase event.
This is possible because your % of the supply you own never changes, in fact, it will only go up, because of the burn system.

This enables new trading strategies, it has also changed the entire dynamic behind DeFi. The most savvy traders will not only make decisions based on prices, but will consider supply, which can bring huge benefits. As always, please do your own research.
